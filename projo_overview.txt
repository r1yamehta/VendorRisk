VendorRisk AI
Comprehensive Third-Party Risk Intelligence Platform

1. Problem Statement
In the modern interconnected digital economy, organizations rely on thousands of
third-party vendors for critical operations, ranging from SaaS platforms to supply
chain logistics. However, this reliance creates a massive "Visibility Gap."
The Core Challenges:
Static Assessments: Traditional risk management relies on manual
questionnaires sent once a year. A vendor's security posture can change in
minutes due to a new CVE (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures).
The "Hidden" Attack Surface: Organizations often don't know the full tech stack
of their vendors. An unpatched Nginx server at a small vendor can become an
entry point into a large corporate network.
Lack of Benchmarking: Decision-makers often see risk scores in a vacuum. They
lack a "Standard Value" or industry benchmark to understand if a vendor is
genuinely risky or just standard for their sector.

2. Solution & Motive

The Motive: To move from reactive "Point-in-Time" assessments to proactive,
data-driven "Comparative Intelligence."

VendorRisk AI acts as a real-time security auditor. It leverages automated technical
discovery and historical data to provide a 360-degree view of vendor risk. Unlike
traditional tools, it uses a Comparability Factor logic.
