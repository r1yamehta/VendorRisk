Why Vendor Risk Intelligence Matters

Data Breaches

Third-party vendors are a leading cause of data breaches, exposing sensitive information and damaging reputations.

Compliance Risks

Failure to ensure vendor compliance with regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and industry standards can lead to hefty fines.

Supply Chain Attacks

Cyber attackers frequently target weaker points in the supply chain to gain access to primary organizations.

Vendor Reliability

Operational disruptions from unreliable vendors can severely impact your business continuity and customer trust.
